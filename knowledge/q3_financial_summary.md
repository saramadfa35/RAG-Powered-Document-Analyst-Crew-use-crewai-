# Nimbus Cloud Solutions — Q3 2026 Financial Summary (Internal)

## 1. Revenue Overview

Total revenue for Q3 2026 (July–September) was **$8.4 million**, compared to
**$7.1 million** in Q2 2026, representing a **quarter-over-quarter increase of 18.3%**.

This growth was primarily attributed to the launch of NimbusSync Pro v4.2 and its
SmartRoute Sync Engine feature in mid-August, which drove a 27% increase in new
Enterprise-tier contract signings during the quarter, particularly from EU and
MENA-based logistics customers.

## 2. Revenue Breakdown by Tier

| Tier        | Q2 2026 Revenue | Q3 2026 Revenue | Change   |
|-------------|-----------------|-----------------|----------|
| Starter     | $1.2M           | $1.3M           | +8.3%    |
| Growth      | $2.6M           | $2.9M           | +11.5%   |
| Enterprise  | $3.3M           | $4.2M           | +27.3%   |

## 3. Operating Expenses

Total operating expenses in Q3 2026 were $6.1 million, up from $5.6 million in Q2,
largely due to increased cloud infrastructure costs from the multi-region SmartRoute
deployment and a hiring push in Customer Success to support Enterprise onboarding.

## 4. Headcount

Headcount grew from 198 employees at the end of Q2 2026 to 214 employees at the end
of Q3 2026, an increase of 16 employees, primarily in Engineering (Lisbon hub) and
Customer Success (Amman hub).

## 5. Net Income

Q3 2026 net income was $1.6 million, compared to $0.9 million in Q2 2026, driven by
the revenue growth outpacing the increase in operating expenses.

## 6. Outlook

Management expects continued Enterprise-tier growth into Q4 2026 as more customers
migrate to NimbusSync Pro v4.2, though infrastructure costs are expected to rise
further with the planned launch of a new APAC regional data center.
