# Nimbus Cloud Solutions — Employee Handbook

## 1. Company Overview

Nimbus Cloud Solutions is a B2B SaaS company founded in 2019, headquartered in Austin, Texas,
with remote engineering hubs in Lisbon and Amman. We provide cloud storage orchestration
software for mid-market logistics companies. As of Q3 2026, we employ 214 people across
12 countries.

## 2. Remote Work Policy

Nimbus operates on a **hybrid-first policy** effective January 2025. Employees are classified
into three work modes:

- **Remote-first**: Employees hired into fully remote roles (primarily Engineering and
  Customer Success) may work from anywhere within their contracted country of employment.
  They are required to be online during a 4-hour daily overlap window with their team's
  core timezone.
- **Hybrid**: Employees based within 50km of an office hub (Austin, Lisbon, Amman) are
  expected to be in-office a minimum of 2 days per week, chosen flexibly in coordination
  with their manager.
- **On-site**: Roles in Facilities, Executive Assistance, and Hardware QA are required to
  be on-site 5 days per week due to the nature of the work.

Employees requesting a full-time remote arrangement outside their hired country must submit
a Cross-Border Work Request to People Operations at least 60 days in advance, subject to tax
and legal review. Approval is not guaranteed and depends on entity registration in the target
country.

All remote and hybrid employees are provided a one-time $600 home-office stipend and a
recurring $40/month connectivity allowance.

## 3. Working Hours & Time Off

Standard working hours are 9:00 AM–5:00 PM in the employee's local timezone, with flexible
start times between 7:00 AM and 10:00 AM. Nimbus offers unlimited PTO with a mandatory
minimum of 15 days taken per calendar year. All employees receive 12 paid company holidays
and 10 paid sick days annually.

## 4. Code of Conduct

All employees must complete annual security awareness training and sign the Data Handling
Agreement. Confidential company information, including financial results prior to public
disclosure, product roadmaps, and customer data, must never be shared outside the
organization or on personal social media accounts.

## 5. Performance Reviews

Performance reviews occur twice yearly (June and December). Ratings range from
"Needs Improvement" to "Exceptional" and are tied to the annual merit increase cycle
each January.
